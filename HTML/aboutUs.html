About Us

VOrgreens is an Indonesia's plant-based eatery chain serving delicious healthy, ethical, and sustainable food. VOrgreens provides many variant cuisines that are undoubtedly healthy and delicious. Scattered throughout Indonesia, VOrgreens serve healthy cuisines equipped with astonishing taste in hope of reducing the use of animal-delivered products.

History

VOrgreens journey began on June 28th, 2012 in the city of Jakarta. Started of as a family operated business, Santoso’s family work in a small kitchen serving plant-based cuisine to local population. As time went on, VOrgreens began to thrive building its brand focused on the idea of serving Indonesia’s delicious healthy, ethical, and sustainable food. Today, we can proudly say that we have expand our menu and services throught the country. We strive to stay true to our customers by providing healthy delicious food with high-quality ingredients to serve a memorable experience.

Our Vision

To be the best plant-based eatery chain in Indonesia that promotes tasty traditional food. As well as, providing exceptional services and creating enjoyable eating atmosphere.

Our Mission

1

Serve high quality plant-based food with fresh ingredients

2

Provide unique dining experience with astonishing service

3

Share Indonesia’s traditional food to the world
